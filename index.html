Doggie Daycare Evaluation

Safety is our number one priority. In order to ensure their safety and the safety of our other guests, each dog is given a two-part evaluation before being permitted to join group play sessions. Pets who prefer to spend time alone will receive customized, individual play sessions so they can still get the most out of their stay!

Our large open areas feature:

  • Spacious indoor and outdoor yards
  • Multiple play structures and yards for all sized dogs with enough room to zoom for all energy levels
  • Raised beds throughout for those that need a break from playing
  • Ample water bowls and overhangs to provide shade
  • Pool parties in our in-ground, bone-shaped swimming pools

*Your pet must be spayed or neutered to participate in All Day Play. For pets who are not spayed or neutered, our Day board option will be available. Please contact the resort for additional information.

Puppy Preschool

Socialization. Potty Training. Good Manners. FUN! Dogs between the ages of 3 and 6 months old will learn basic manners and social skills. Our program is dedicated to teaching your youngster important life skills such as crate training, problem-solving, and puppy etiquette


Your puppy will be cared for our loving, out-of-this-world team while they learn the puppy basics! 3 personalized play times provide a space for your puppy to learn basic manners and social queues with people and their puppy peers. And we mentioned fun, so our interactive toys keep them occupied for hours while also stimulating their young minds! Extended check-out time of 8pm is available to our Puppy Preschool students. The Puppy Preschool program is available 7 days a week from 6:30am-8pm.


We help teach puppy etiquitte through:

  • Play sessions with other puppies to practice gentle play and socialization
  • Crate training and potty walks spread throughout the day to help with potty training and leash experience
  • Outside breaks at least 6 times a day for fresh air and to gain confidence in new surroundings
  • Interactive games and toys to develop problem-solving skills.

Puppy Prescholl Daily Schedule:

  • 6:30am: Social or Individual Potty Walk
  • 10am: Social Play Session (20-30 min)
  • 12:30pm: Social Play Session (20-30 min)
  • 3pm: Social Play Session (20-30 min)
  • 5:30pm: Social Potty Walk
  • 7:30pm: Social or Individual Potty Walk
